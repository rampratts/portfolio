hello.

I'm a full stack developer located in Uruguay. I love working with technology and developing new stuff. I'm passionate about programming and most of the work I've done is as a hobby, spending quite a lot of my free time programming and learning new technologies.

I've always loved technology. I grew up with a computer, which I used since I was 2 years old (nowadays is a common thing to see kids with electronic devices, but back then it wasn't very common!). My passion for computers led me to be curious about how they work, and when I got access to the internet I started looking for all my answers. First I searched how to create videogames, and I even made one when I was 10. Then, at 11 years old I wrote my first Java program, a simple Hello world, but since then, I knew it was what I wanted to do for the rest of my life.

Now at 19 years old, I'm still learning and creating new things. I've made a couple of games with Unity, but now I work mostly with web technologies. I'm a self-learner, willing to always learn more and challenge myself with new projects and technologies. If you'd like to talk to me, go to the contact section and send me an email about whatever you want, I'm open to having a talk with anybody about anything. I'll contact you as soon as possible.
